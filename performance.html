PERFORMANCES


Students have performed at several locations in India and abroad. They have performed outside India in Singapore and also performed for other organisations like ISKCON, Mandala Pooja, Durga Pooja, Swadeshi Mela, Heritage Melaetc.


OTHER ASSOCIATIONS

Our Association is associated with various regional and national associations like Dwarka Durga Pooja Mandal, Dwarka Ayyappa Sewa Organisation, Association of Ladies Neighborhood, Heritage Mela, Swadeshi Mela, ISKCON, Delhi Flower Show etc. to name a few.

International level Association:

Our Association is also associated with 21 Gun Salute Heritage & Cultural Trustsince 2012 and Nrutyadham Kala Samity since 2019.
