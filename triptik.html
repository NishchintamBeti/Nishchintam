ACHARYA TRIPTI KALRA

keyboard_arrow_left Previous keyboard_arrow_right Next

Smt. Tripti Kalra, although born and brought up in North Indian Sikh family, worships Indian Classical Dance and has innate love for the art form. Smt. Tripti Kalra is a disciple of Bharatnatyam exponent and Guru Smt. Kanaka Sudhakar. She has done her Visharad from Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal, Miraj (Mumbai). She completed her Masters from Indira Kala Sangeet Vishwavidyalaya, Khairagarh, is pursuing her PhD from Rabindra Bharati University, Kolkata and working as Asstt. Professor of Performing Arts at Delhi Teacher's Training College, G.G.S. Indraprastha University, Delhi Smt. Tripti Kalra performed extensively all over India. Her forte is her fluid expression, crisp footwork and intense involvement. She has choreographed and presented many innovative items in Bharatnatyam and won appreciation. She is training more than 100 students in her academy. She has been recently affiliated with Guru Samman Vibhuti at the Singapore Festival held in May 2019.
